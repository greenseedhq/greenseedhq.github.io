TEDxUCL is a student organised TED event bringing together nine hundred attendees and leading speakers at the world’s fifth best rated university*. The event each year is focused around a common theme. Growth is this year’s theme. From personal growth right through to global and environmental, this event will take us on a journey through the different ways in which we can grow. As as individuals, as communities, and as humanity, we are always evolving, and it is important to think about how we do so. Speakers will explore themes of innovation, equality, sustainability, and more. The talks aim to explore and offer insight into how how we can progress in a positive way, and have a positive impact.
